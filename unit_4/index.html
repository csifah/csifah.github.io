A little about us...

Afrolish is a place where you can come and belong. Growing up in the UK, first generation with Ghanaina parents I had to learn to blend cultures and discover who I was and where I fit. Add on being gay and that can be a head f*ck. Rather than try to belong I found my own path and along the way found many interests and interesting peoplealong the way. Here is an almagimation of our adventures - things that have made us laugh, cry, made as angry and given us lots to discuss. Hopefully it will give you somewhere to belong, things to discuss and a few laughs when needed.
